Privacy Policy

NailCut Reminder Tracker  ·  Last updated: June 2026

Your privacy matters. This page explains what data NailCut Reminder Tracker collects — and the short answer is: almost none.

What data we collect

NailCut Reminder Tracker does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information. There are no user accounts, no sign-ups, and no servers.

All data you enter — your trim dates and reminder preferences — is stored locally on your device only. We never see it.

Third-party services

The app does not use any third-party analytics, advertising SDKs, or tracking tools. No data is shared with any third party.

Notifications

If you enable reminders, the app uses Apple's local notification system to send alerts directly on your device. No data leaves your device as part of this process.

Children's privacy

The app does not collect personal information from anyone, including children under 13.

Changes to this policy

If anything changes, we'll update this page. The date at the top will reflect the latest revision.

Questions? Reach us at support@nailcuttracker.com
